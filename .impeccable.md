# AdPilot Design Context

## Target Audience
Marketing agency operators and their teams managing client social media campaigns. Mix of agency owners (business-focused, want results at a glance) and content managers (detail-oriented, live in the tool daily).

## Use Cases
- Managing campaigns across 9 social platforms from one dashboard
- Creating and scheduling social media posts with AI assistance
- Reviewing analytics and performance metrics
- Connecting and managing platform OAuth integrations
- Team collaboration with approval workflows

## Brand Personality
**Command center energy.** Think Bloomberg Terminal meets modern SaaS — data-dense but clean. Confident, not flashy. The tool should feel like a weapon, not a toy. Dark theme default with sharp accents. Typography should be tight and editorial.

## Design Direction
- **Tone**: Industrial-editorial. Dense information, clean lines, no wasted space.
- **Differentiation**: The sidebar should feel like mission control. Data should be scannable at a glance. AI features should feel seamlessly integrated, not bolted on.
- **Typography**: Sharp geometric sans-serif. Tight tracking on headings.
- **Color**: Dark slate base, electric blue accent (#3b82f6), warm amber for warnings, emerald for success. Muted backgrounds, vivid accents.
